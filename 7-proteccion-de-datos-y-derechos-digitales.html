Texto

🔐 Digital Safety and Responsible Use
When working on this project, you must follow these rules to protect your personal data and respect digital rights:

 
🔒 Protect your personal information
- Do not share personal data (name, address, phone number, etc.)
- Do not upload private images without permission
 
🌐 Use safe and reliable sources
- Use trusted websites
- Check the information before using it
 
📚 Respect copyright
- Do not copy and paste information
- Use your own words
- Mention your sources
 
💬 Be responsible online
- Use digital tools respectfully
- Do not share inappropriate content
- Be kind and respectful to others
 
💡 Remember


Your digital identity is important. Protect it!
